Introduction

Topheman Bombs is a free HTML5 / Javascript game that takes advantage of the canvas element and the accelerometer on your device.

  • Tilt your device to manage the blue dot
  • Tap the screen to drop bombs on the enemies (red dots)
  • Grab the green dots to get more bombs

Story

Topheman Bombs has been written so that it could be played on any platform with the same source code.

Developpement : Platforms

  • iOs (iPhone/iPad) : Browser mode playing (I didn't made an app because I don't have any mac computer nor 100$/year to put in the Apple developper license)
  • Android : I made a webApp with phonegap, available on the android market (also, the accelerometer not being accessible through Javascript in Android browser, you can't play it directly in the browser)
  • Desktop computers : Some laptops such as the macbook have accelerometers, so you can play Topheman Bombs on your laptop, tilting it. If it hasn't any accelerometer, you can play with an emulator : remote-tilt, which I used during developpement (I'll explain it in a blog post)

Developpement : Ball.js

At the beginning of the project, I developped a class Ball.js that supports all the physics (ball collisions, movements ...) and display effects (color, alpha, glowing, blinking, explosions ...) I needed for my game which was still at the time a work in progress.

Now that I finished Topheman Bombs, I can release Ball.js to the open-source, it could be usefull to developpers who would like to make animations or games such as pool billard, air hockey ...
